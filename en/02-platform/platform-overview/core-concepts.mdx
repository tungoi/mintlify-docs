**Why This Matters**

<Info>
  To build truly powerful and flexible business systems, you must first change your thinking. Instead of searching for the "perfect app" that forces you into a rigid box, Luklak provides you with a set of universal "LEGO pieces". By understanding these core components, you shift from being a simple user to becoming an architect, capable of constructing any solution your business needs with unlimited flexibility.
</Info>

**The 9 Core LEGO Pieces**

These nine components are the atomic building blocks of the entire Luklak platform. They are designed to work together seamlessly, allowing you to model, manage, and automate any process.
