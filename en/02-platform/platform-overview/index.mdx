## Layer 1: The Build Layer (Constructing Your Skyscraper)

This is the layer of infinite potential, where you design and construct your unique operational skyscraper, floor by floor. The process follows a logical 3-tier structure.

  <Card title="Tier 3: The Live Building (Spaces, Workspaces & Homes)" icon="building" iconType="duotone">
    This is the finished skyscraper where work happens. ## Layer 2: The Ground Layer (The Land and The Law)

Every great skyscraper needs a solid foundation and clear rules. The Ground Layer provides the secure, governed plot of land upon which your entire structure is built.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="The Land & Its People" icon="users" iconType="duotone">
    This is where you populate your digital land. First, you define its inhabitants: individual `Users`, the `Groups` they form (like departments), and the `Roles` they play (their jobs). Then, you grant each **`User`** their **Business Privilege**‚Äîthe official "passport" that allows them to enter your property.
  </Card>
  <Card title="The Law of the Land" icon="key" iconType="duotone">
    This layer provides the specific security and rules for your property. It includes the keys to each building block, blueprint, floor, and room (**Item Access Control**) and the detailed rules of conduct within them (**Permission Schemes**), ensuring your headquarters is both accessible and secure.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
