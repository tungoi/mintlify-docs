## **OVERVIEW**

<Tip>
  **General Description**

  - **Purpose:** To manage the execution of interior design and construction projects, ensuring they are completed on time, within budget, and to the required quality standards.
  - **Internal Business Logic:** Manages a `Project` from start to finish, including all associated `Tasks`. It automatically calculates project progress based on the completion of child tasks.
  - **Business Impact:** Ensures consistent project quality, improves client satisfaction, and provides clear visibility into the progress of all ongoing work.
</Tip>

**Inputs & Outputs**

- **Inputs:** `CRM.Customer` (specifically, when a deal is won, via Global Rule **#CF-03**).
- **Outputs:** `Accounting.Invoice` (for final billing, via Global Rule **#CF-04**).
