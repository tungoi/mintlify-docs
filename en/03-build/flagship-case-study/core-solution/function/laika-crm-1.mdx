## **OVERVIEW**

<Tip>
  **General Description**

  - **Purpose:** To manage potential customers from lead acquisition to deal closure and handover to subsequent departments.
  - **Internal Business Logic:** Manages the lifecycle of a potential customer through stages like New Lead, Contacting, and Appointment Set. Includes logic for creating and managing Demo `ðŸ§Šï¸Ž Sub-Objects` (appointments). Automatically assigns new customers to sales staff.
  - **Business Impact:** Increases lead conversion rates through a standardized and closely monitored consultation and follow-up process.
</Tip>

**Inputs & Outputs**

- **Inputs:** `Marketing.Ad` (via Global Rule **#CF-01**).
- **Outputs:** `Accounting.Contract`, `Project Delivery.Site Survey` (via Global Rule **#CF-03**).
- **External Interaction:** `Google Calendar` (via Global Rule **#CF-02**).
