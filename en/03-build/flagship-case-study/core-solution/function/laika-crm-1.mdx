#### **OVERVIEW**

**General Description**

- **Purpose:** To manage potential customers from lead acquisition to deal closure and handover to subsequent departments.
- **Internal Business Logic:** Manages the lifecycle of a potential customer through stages like New Lead, Contacting, and Appointment Set. Includes logic for creating and managing Demo `ðŸ§Šï¸Ž Sub-Objects` (appointments). Automatically assigns new customers to sales staff.
- **Business Impact:** Increases lead conversion rates through a standardized and closely monitored consultation and follow-up process.

**Inputs & Outputs**

- **Inputs:** `Marketing.Ad` (via Global Rule **#CF-01**).
- **Outputs:** `Accounting.Contract`, `Project Delivery.Site Survey` (via Global Rule **#CF-03**).
- **External Interaction:** `Google Calendar` (via Global Rule **#CF-02**).

##### **1. Customer (Standard Object Type)**

- **Process Description**: Manages potential customers from initial contact through the sales pipeline.
- **Example**: "Anh B - Vinhomes Smart City", "Chá»‹ C - Royal City".

**Workflow: Customer Lifecycle**

| Status                | Available Transitions                                                                       |
| :-------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **NEW LEAD**          | `Start Contact` â†’ MOVES TO `CONTACTING`                                                     |

##### **2. Demo (Sub-Object Type)**

- **Process Description**: Manages demo appointments with customers. A `Demo` is created when a `Customer` status is moved to `APPOINTMENT SET`.
- **Example**: "Demo with Anh B - 10 AM 20/08/2025".

**Workflow: Demo Lifecycle**

| Status        | Available Transitions                                                    |
| :------------ | :----------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **SCHEDULED** | `Complete` â†’ MOVES TO `COMPLETED` \<br\> `Cancel` â†’ MOVES TO `CANCELLED` |
| **COMPLETED** | (End Status)                                                             |
| **CANCELLED** | (End Status)                                                             |

**Data Fields**

| Field Name         | Field Type  | Description                                                   |
| :----------------- | :---------- | :------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Demo Time          | Date & Time | The scheduled date and time for the demo.                     |
| Location           | Short Text  | The location of the appointment (Online or physical address). |
| Attending Designer | User        | The designer who will join the sales rep for the demo.        |
